Extended Producer Responsibility

Extended Producer's Responsibility (EPR) is the main feature of the E-waste (Management and Handling) Rules, 2011, wherein the producers of electrical and electronic equipment are given the responsibility of managing such equipments even after its 'end of life', thus the producer is responsible for their products once the consumer discards them. Under this EPR, the producer is also entrusted with the responsibility to finance and organize a system to meet the costs involved in complying with the EPR.

We will strive to create awareness amongst our consumers about managing E-waste, protecting the environment, and most importantly - for following the recycling and take-back process of end of life products.

As 'producer', we shall make arrangements to

  • Collect and channelize E-waste generated in repairing activity and 'end of life' of the product.

  • Create all round awareness through publications & information dissemination.

  • Maintain records & file annual returns.
